Down the River Family Fun

Making our way down the river with families is what we do best. We love what we do and are sure you will too. With family fun and safety at the forefront of our work, you can plan on great family fun every day with our certified and exceptional guides. As you are floating your way down the river, be sure to enjoy all of the peace and beauty that this area offers. Admire the grand rocks, beautiful blue waters, wild life in the middle of their natural habitat, and the fresh breezes to relax you. We invite your family to come relax with us on one of the amazing rivers we visit. Check out our trip packages and let us know which one best suits your family and your goals for your fun and relaxing vacation. We can't wait to hear from you. We look forward to serving you.

History

We've been creating fun-filled family rafting advenures since 1959. What started out as a way to spend great summers in nature together as a family turned into a passion our family wanted to share with others. We've been doing it ever since.
